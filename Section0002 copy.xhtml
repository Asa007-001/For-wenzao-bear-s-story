Ch1: The Origin of Wenzao Bear

Wenzao Bear was designed by Wenzao Ursuline University of Languages alumnus Tseng Wen-Yan and donated to his alma mater as a charity merchandise item.

The bear wears an outfit inspired by Wenzao's school uniform, with “Serviam” embroidered on its cuffs—symbolizing the spirit of Wenzao: “I will serve.”

In addition, Wenzao Bear's paws are designed with vibrant colors, highlighting its cuteness and approachability.

Standing approximately 40 centimeters tall, the bear embodies warmth and inclusiveness, becoming a beloved campus symbol among Wenzao's faculty and students.

Ch2: The Unique Features of Wenzao Bear

Wenzao Bear is not just an adorable mascot—it also embodies the spirit of public service and campus culture at Wenzao Ursuline University of Languages.

This edition of Wenzao Bear was originally launched as a charity sale item, with proceeds supporting initiatives such as the "Disadvantaged Student Assistance Program" and the "Overseas Education Fund." It symbolizes the core spirit of Wenzao students—their willingness to serve.

During events such as the school anniversary and alumni homecoming, Wenzao Bear is often presented by the university as a special gift to returning alumni. For instance, in the “Back to 18” event, the president personally gifted Wenzao Bear to participants, symbolizing cherished memories, heartfelt blessings, and the emotional bond shared among the Wenzao community.

Ch3:The Design and Symbolism of Wenzao Bear

The design of Wenzao Bear blends the spirit of the campus with the vibrancy of youth.

It wears a plaid skirt and top inspired by the Wenzao school uniform, reflecting both a student-like charm and a collegiate style.

Embroidered on the bear's sleeve is the word “Serviam,” which means “I will serve.”

This is not only one of Wenzao's mottos—it also represents the spirit of Wenzao students, who face the world with a heart ready to serve and a willingness to help others.

Wenzao Bear's paws are designed in vibrant colors, adding a sense of warmth and energy that makes it the most popular photo companion at campus events.

As a symbol of alumni donations and school anniversary gifts, Wenzao Bear also represents the emotional bond among alumni—allowing Wenzao members to feel the warmth of their alma mater, no matter where they are in the world.

Ch4: Fun Little Stories About Wenzao Bear

On Wenzao's campus, a small legend about “service and warmth” has been passed down.

Many years ago, a little bear was born in the camphor forest behind the campus.

It has a pair of soft paws and always enjoys helping others.

Whether it's picking up handouts blown away by the wind,

Or gently helping up a classmate who has fallen,

The little bear always smiles and says, “I will serve!”

One day, the little bear met an alumnus visiting their alma mater in the library—

The alumnus was once a student in the German Department and had a passion for design.

He was deeply moved by the little bear's kindness and decided to capture its image in a drawing.

And turned it into an adorable bear plushie dressed in the Wenzao school uniform.

He embroidered “Serviam” on the bear's sleeve—a Latin word meaning “I will serve.”

From that day on, the bear was affectionately known as “Wenzao Bear.”

It embodies the spirit of Wenzao students:

  1. Willing to help others
  2. Embracing diversity
  3. Spreading warmth through a smile

Today, when you see Wenzao Bear around campus,

Perhaps it's gently reminding you—

“Service is not an obligation, but a kind of happiness.”

Ch5:Summary

Wenzao Bear is not only an adorable campus mascot, but also a symbol of spirit.

It represents the core values of Wenzao students—service, care, sharing, and warmth.

From the designer's heartfelt intention and the school's charitable purpose to the emotional bond between students and alumni,

The birth of Wenzao Bear has woven together every thread of love and memory.

Every hug, every charity sale, every photo taken together,

Each moment brings the bear's story to life and carries forward the spirit of “Serviam.”

Wenzao Bear reminds us:

Even after leaving the campus, the warmth of Wenzao people continues to shine in every corner.

It is not just a symbol of Wenzao, but a shared memory for everyone who has learned and grown here.
